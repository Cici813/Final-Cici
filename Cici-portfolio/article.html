The primary objective of this design project is to create a visually stunning and cohesive theme for a hi-end special poker card set that resonates with luxury, sophistication, and exclusivity. The design aims to appeal to both avid poker players and collectors, making the cards highly desirable as a premium and unique collector's item

Inspired by the world view, characters and elements of the original God game itself, designers are also encouraged to add their own creativity and fresh perspective to the design to exceed customer expectations and create a truly unique and amazing original God themed playing card set that brings more fun and value to players and collectors
